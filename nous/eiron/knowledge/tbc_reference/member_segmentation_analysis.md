*Building on Stephen's discovery call insights + competitive intelligence*

## Base Data (Discovery Call Confirmed)

**Total Membership**: 175 (110 companies, 70 individuals)  
**Retention Rate**: 87% (2024), targeting 90%+  
**Revenue Pattern**: ~99.6% from program services (dues + events)

## Tier Economics (Stephen's Analysis)

### **Strategic Tier ($25K) - "Sweet Spot"**
- **Profile**: Moderate engagement, reasonable support asks
- **Value**: Most profitable tier - good revenue without high maintenance
- **Growth target**: Stephen confirmed this is ideal expansion focus

### **Executive Tier ($50K) - "High Maintenance"** 
- **Profile**: Largest individual revenue contribution
- **Support burden**: "Ask for a ton", extensive support required
- **Economics**: High revenue but high cost-to-serve

### **Startup Tier (3% revenue model) - "High Ask, Low Revenue"**
- **Profile**: Only 3% of overall TBC revenue
- **Behavior**: "Ask for a lot" - want connections with major miners
- **Economics**: Low revenue, high engagement demands
- **Strategic value**: Ecosystem growth, future potential

### **Individual Members (70 total) - "Coalition Optics"**
- **Profile**: Supporters, advocates, some industry professionals  
- **ERCOT value**: Minimal (no MW capacity)
- **Strategic value**: Coalition legitimacy, grassroots appearance

## Economics Model (Preliminary)

### **Revenue Optimization**
- **Strategic Tier Expansion**: Focus growth on $25K sweet spot
- **Executive Tier Management**: Improve cost-to-serve ratio
- **Bespoke Services**: New revenue stream for networking-only members

### **ERCOT Voting Optimization** 
- **Quantity Strategy**: Many small miners = more votes than few large miners
- **Retention Priority**: Small miners (1-50 MW) for vote durability
- **Large Miner Risk**: AI transition threatens both revenue and votes
