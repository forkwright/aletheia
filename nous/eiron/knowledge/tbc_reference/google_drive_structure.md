## Rationale for Structure

**Executive Summary First**: Busy stakeholders (TBC leadership, professor) can access key findings immediately

**Analysis Foundation**: Core deliverables in professional format with clear supporting documentation

**Strategic Options**: Decision-ready materials with implementation guidance

**TBC Coordination**: Central location for all client-facing materials and feedback

**Academic Deliverables**: Clean separation of academic requirements from business analysis

**Team Coordination**: Integration point for Derek's workstreams without interference

## Integration with Derek's Team Structure

**Our Contribution**: Analysis Foundation + Strategic Options (analytical backbone)

**Derek's Workstreams**: 
- WS3 (Competitive) → Integrate our competitive intelligence
- WS4 (Culture/Identity) → Reference our strategic options  
- Team coordination → Use our TBC data requests and interview framework

**Avoid Duplication**: Our ERCOT analysis stands alone (Aaron absorbed), competitive intelligence supplements but doesn't replace Derek's approach

## File Naming Conventions

**Format**: YYYY-MM-DD_DocumentType_Version
**Examples**:
- 2026-01-29_Competitive_Intelligence_v1.0.docx
- 2026-02-05_Strategic_Options_v2.1.xlsx
- 2026-02-15_TBC_Meeting_Summary.docx

**Version Control**: Major revisions (1.0, 2.0), Minor edits (1.1, 1.2)

## Access Permissions

**Full Team Access**: All folders except 06_Working_Files (our workspace)
**TBC Access**: 00_Executive_Summary, 03_TBC_Coordination only
**Professor Access**: 00_Executive_Summary, 04_Academic_Deliverables
