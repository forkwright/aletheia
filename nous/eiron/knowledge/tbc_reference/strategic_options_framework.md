## Three Strategic Options

### **Option A: Bitcoin Maximalist**
*Prioritize mining community cohesion and Bitcoin policy purity*

#### **Member Focus**
- **Core**: Small-medium Bitcoin miners (1-100 MW)
- **Tier Optimization**: Strategic ($25K) + Individual supporters
- **Geographic**: Texas-first, selective out-of-state mining

#### **Value Proposition** 
- Pure Bitcoin advocacy (no "shitcoin" compromise)
- Texas mining rights protection (Right to Mine legislation)
- Regulatory defense against ESG attacks
- Authentic mining community (not corporate theater)

#### **ERCOT Strategy**
- **Quantity over scale**: Many small miners = more durable votes
- **Voting power**: High resilience (100 miners √ó 5 MW vs 1 miner √ó 500 MW)

#### **Competitive Position**

- **Advantage**: Texas policy expertise + local network

## Strategic Recommendation

### **Recommended: Option B (AI-Inclusive Growth)**
*Narrow margin based on industry trajectory and competitive positioning*

#### **Rationale**
1. **Industry Reality**: Mining-to-AI transition is economic imperative ($25/kWh AI vs $1/kWh BTC)
2. **Competitive Advantage**: Get ahead of Data Center Coalition before they organize
3. **ERCOT Influence**: Large MW has increasing policy relevance as grid scales
4. **Revenue Optimization**: Growth trajectory supports TBC's financial sustainability

#### **Implementation Strategy**
1. **Phase 1 (90 days)**: Secure transitioning members (Core Scientific, etc.) before competitor capture
2. **Phase 2 (6 months)**: Launch AI/HPC policy initiative, expand service offerings  
3. **Phase 3 (12 months)**: Build AI infrastructure coalition, Texas tech hub narrative

#### **Risk Mitigation**
- **Bitcoin member retention**: Create Bitcoin-specific advocacy track within broader mission
- **Identity management**: Frame as "Texas energy infrastructure" vs abandoning Bitcoin
- **Competitive response**: Move fast before Data Center Coalition establishes market position

## Explicit Sacrifices

### **What TBC Must Accept (Option B)**

- **Revenue Decline**: Financial sustainability crisis  
- **Executive Partner Loss**: Riot, MARA, Core Scientific defection
- **Policy Irrelevance**: Missing the AI/energy infrastructure wave
