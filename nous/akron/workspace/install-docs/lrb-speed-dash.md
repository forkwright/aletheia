## Detailed Dash Removal Process

*Based on Great Lakes 4x4 forum technical guide for 94-97 Ram*

### Required Tools
- 13mm socket
- Phillips screwdriver
- Small flat screwdriver (eyeglass type)
- 7mm socket
- 8mm socket
- 10mm socket
- Size 20 torx bit
- 1.5-2 foot extension bar
- Rivet gun (for assembly)

### Step-by-Step Removal

#### Pre-Work
1. **Disconnect battery(s)** - MANDATORY for safety
2. Remove kick panel under steering column
3. Unscrew hood latch and parking brake release handles - let hang

#### Interior Work
4. **Parking brake assembly**: Remove 3x 13mm bolts from firewall, slide 2" toward passenger side
5. **Wiring disconnection**: 
   - 5-7 connectors under dash (varies by options)
   - Small ground wire on parking brake assembly
   - 3-5 plugs on driver side
   - Large white box connector (10mm bolt)
   - 2 connectors on passenger side under glovebox
   - Antenna wire (pull out 4" and disconnect)

#### Structural Components
6. **Remove kick panels/door sills** (may need drilling if rusted)
7. **Airbag module**: Remove center cover (2 screws), then module (4x 10mm bolts from floor)
8. **Additional bolt**: Remove 8mm bolt to right of airbag module

#### Engine Bay Work
9. **Automatic transmission**: Unclip shifter linkage at firewall joint
10. **Steering**: Remove 13mm bolt, pop steering shaft off column base
11. **Steering column**: Remove 3x 13mm nuts from firewall (use extension)

#### Final Interior Removal
12. **Steering column plastics**: Remove with 20mm torx
13. **Wiring**: Unbolt clip from turn signal switch, disconnect airbag at yellow connector
14. **Column removal**: Remove 2x 10mm bolts, drop and remove column
15. **A-pillar covers**: Remove carefully (clips - pull toward door openings)
16. **Center console**: Remove ash tray, cup holder assemblies
17. **Dash bezel**: Remove (clips only), then heater controls (4 screws)
18. **Heater connections**: Disconnect electrical, heater cable (red push tab), vacuum lines
19. **Top screws**: Remove all 8mm screws along windshield
20. **Final checks**: Disconnect brake light switch, verify all connections free

### Before Starting
1. **Verify all components** against parts list
2. **Test fit major pieces** before final assembly
3. **Plan wiring routes** before installation
4. **Take photos** of original wiring for reference

### During Installation

### Post-Installation
