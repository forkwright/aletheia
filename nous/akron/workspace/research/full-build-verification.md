## P7100 MODIFICATIONS

### Fuel Plate (#10 vs #11)
| Plate | Characteristics | Best For |
|-------|-----------------|----------|
| **#10** | More aggressive early fuel, stronger hit | Performance, play truck |
| **#11** | Smoother ramp, controlled delivery | Towing, daily driver, work |
| Both | Similar max fuel, ~200-280 HP potential | Depends on supporting mods |

**For your 46RE + towing use:** #11 plate recommended
