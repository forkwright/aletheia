## SAFETY RECALLS - NHTSA

### Critical Recalls for 1997 Model Year (11 Total)

**1. NHTSA Campaign: 01V153000 - AIRBAG CLOCKSPRING**
- **Issue:** Sound deadening material inside steering wheel can detach and interfere with clockspring ribbon, causing open circuit
- **Effect:** Driver airbag system disabled, warning lamp illuminated
- **Remedy:** Replace clockspring assembly
- **Status:** üîç **NEEDS INVESTIGATION** - User has new clockspring installed, verify if recall-compliant part
- **Vehicles Affected:** 216,100 (including 1997-2001 models)

**2. NHTSA Campaign: 01V040000 - HOOD LATCH**
- **Issue:** Corrosion of return spring on hood secondary latch (Salt Belt states only)
- **Effect:** Hood could open unexpectedly if primary latch not engaged  
- **Remedy:** Replace secondary hood latch
- **Status:** üîç **NEEDS INVESTIGATION** - Check hood latch operation
- **Vehicles Affected:** 701,000 (1993-1999 models)

**3. NHTSA Campaign: 97V084000 - TRANSMISSION COOLING LINES**
- **Issue:** Transmission fluid lines can disconnect at high temperatures, spraying fluid on exhaust manifold
- **Effect:** Risk of fire from fluid spraying on exhaust
- **Remedy:** Replace transmission cooling lines
- **Status:** üîç **NEEDS INVESTIGATION** - Inspect transmission cooling lines
- **Vehicles Affected:** 960,000 (1994+ models)

**4. NHTSA Campaign: 02V161000 - THROTTLE LINKAGE (DIESEL ONLY)**
- **Issue:** Throttle linkage joints can corrode, causing throttle to bind or stick
- **Effect:** Loss of throttle control, crash risk
- **Remedy:** Replace rod ends and ball studs
- **Status:** üîç **NEEDS INVESTIGATION** - Check throttle linkage operation
- **Vehicles Affected:** 97,500 diesel trucks

**5. NHTSA Campaign: 99V023000 - AIRBAG UNEXPECTED DEPLOYMENT**
- **Issue:** Air bag electronic control module (under driver seat) can deploy airbags if impacted
- **Effect:** Unexpected airbag deployment causing injury
- **Remedy:** Replace AECM with revised hardware/software
- **Status:** üîç **NEEDS INVESTIGATION** - Check for updated AECM
- **Vehicles Affected:** 250,845

**6. NHTSA Campaign: 99V115000 - CLUTCH HYDRAULIC LINE (MANUAL V10/4WD ONLY)**
- **Issue:** Hydraulic clutch line can rupture under extreme conditions
- **Effect:** Fire risk from fluid on hot exhaust
- **Remedy:** Replace hydraulic clutch assembly
- **Status:** ‚úÖ **NOT APPLICABLE** - Vehicle has automatic transmission

**7-11. AFTERMARKET LIGHTING RECALLS**
- Multiple recalls for aftermarket headlamps/combination lamps
- **Status:** üîç **NEEDS INVESTIGATION** - Check if any aftermarket lighting installed

## TECHNICAL SERVICE BULLETINS (TSBs)

### Engine - 5.9L 12-Valve Cummins

**KDP (Killer Dowel Pin) - CRITICAL**
- **Issue:** Timing gear housing dowel pin can fall out, potentially destroying engine
- **Effect:** Pin can damage timing cover, cam gear, or drop into oil pan
- **Solution:** Tab/secure dowel pin in place during timing cover service  
- **Status:** ‚úÖ **ALREADY ADDRESSED** - KDP kit confirmed installed per vehicle records

**Grid Heater Relay Issues**
- **Issue:** Grid heater relay commonly fails, causing hard starting in cold weather
- **Location:** Relay box under hood, typically 40A relay
- **Solution:** Replace with heavy-duty relay, check grid heater resistance
- **Status:** üîç **NEEDS INVESTIGATION** - Test grid heater operation

**Timing Cover Leaks** 
- **Issue:** Front timing cover seal commonly leaks with age
- **Symptoms:** Oil leak at front of engine, visible on harmonic balancer
- **Solution:** Replace timing cover seal, check harmonic balancer
- **Status:** üîç **NEEDS INVESTIGATION** - Inspect for leaks during steering work

**Lift Pump Failure (ETB Pump Trucks)**
- **Issue:** Mechanical lift pump on ETB-equipped trucks prone to failure
- **Symptoms:** Poor fuel pressure, long cranking, loss of power
- **Solution:** Replace lift pump or upgrade to electric pump
- **Status:** üîç **NEEDS INVESTIGATION** - Test fuel pressure

### Transmission - 46RE Automatic

- **Issue:** OEM solenoids prone to failure, causing harsh shifts or slipping
