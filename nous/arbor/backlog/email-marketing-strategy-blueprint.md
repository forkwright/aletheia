## 2. Customer Lifecycle Automation Flows

- Subject: "Your A2Z Tree Service Quote Request Received"

- CTA: Call for urgent needs

- Subject: "Meet Adam & the A2Z Tree Service Team"

## 6. Compliance & Deliverability Plan

### 6.1 CAN-SPAM Compliance Framework

**Required Elements in Every Email**
- Clear sender identification: "A2Z Tree Service, Galveston, TX"
- Truthful subject lines: No deceptive or misleading content
- Clear identification as advertisement (when applicable)
- Valid physical address: A2Z Tree Service business address
- Clear unsubscribe mechanism: One-click unsubscribe link
- Honor opt-out requests: Process within 10 business days

**Opt-in Process**
- Double opt-in for newsletter subscriptions
- Single opt-in for service-related communications
- Clear expectation setting: frequency and content type
- Service-related emails (quotes, confirmations) exempt from opt-in requirements

**Record Keeping**
- Maintain subscription source records
- Track opt-out requests and processing dates
- Document email send logs and delivery metrics
- Store consent records for business communications

### 6.2 Local Regulations & Considerations

**Texas Business Regulations**
- Comply with Texas Deceptive Trade Practices Act
- Include business license information when applicable
- Honor Texas "Do Not Call" lists for phone follow-ups

**Industry-Specific Compliance**
- Include required insurance and licensing information
- Follow ISA (International Society of Arboriculture) guidelines
- Maintain professional standards in all communications

### 6.3 Deliverability Optimization

**Domain Authentication**
- SPF record setup: Authorize ActiveCampaign to send
- DKIM signing: Cryptographic signature authentication  
- DMARC policy: Specify how to handle authentication failures
- Custom tracking domain: Use subdomain of a2z409.com

**IP Reputation Management**
- Use ActiveCampaign's shared IP pool (proven reputation)
- Monitor sender reputation scores
- Avoid spam complaints through quality content
- Maintain engagement rates above industry standards

**List Hygiene Practices**
- Regular removal of hard bounces (immediate)
- Soft bounce monitoring and removal (after 3 attempts)
- Engagement-based list cleaning (remove non-openers after 6 months)
- Suppression list maintenance for opt-outs and complaints

**Content Quality Standards**
- Avoid spam trigger words ("free," "guaranteed," etc.)
- Maintain text-to-image ratio (60/40 text/images)
- Include alt text for all images
- Test email content with spam detection tools

### 6.4 Data Protection & Privacy

**Customer Data Handling**
- Secure storage of all customer information
- Limited access to authorized personnel only
- Regular data backups and security audits
- Clear privacy policy for email communications

**GDPR Consideration (if applicable)**
- Even though Texas-based, some customers may be EU residents
- Provide clear consent mechanisms
- Allow data access and deletion requests
- Maintain records of consent and data processing
