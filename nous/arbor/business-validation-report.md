## 1. Legal & Insurance Compliance Checklist

### üî¥ IMMEDIATE REQUIREMENTS (Complete in Month 1)

#### Business Formation
- [ ] **File LLC Certificate of Formation** with Texas Secretary of State
  - Cost: $308 + registered agent ($100-300/year)
  - Timeline: 5-7 business days
  - Benefits: Limited liability protection, professional credibility
  - **Risk if not completed:** Personal assets at risk for business liabilities

#### Insurance Coverage (REQUIRED BEFORE ANY WORK)
- [ ] **General Liability Insurance** ($1-2M coverage recommended)
  - Cost: $1,200-3,000/year
  - Essential for property damage protection
- [ ] **Commercial Auto Insurance** (if using vehicles)
  - Must meet Texas minimum: $30K/$60K/$25K
- [ ] **Workers' Compensation** (if hiring employees)
  - Required by Texas law, $2,000-8,000/year
- [ ] **Professional Liability/Errors & Omissions**
  - Cost: $800-2,000/year for consulting work

#### Federal/State Compliance
- [ ] **Obtain Federal EIN** (free through IRS website)
- [ ] **Open business bank account** (requires EIN and LLC docs)
- [ ] **Register for Texas sales tax** (if selling products/materials)

### üü° RECOMMENDED IMPROVEMENTS (Complete in Month 2-3)

#### Professional Certifications
- [ ] **ISA Certified Arborist** (Industry gold standard)
  - Requirements: 3 years experience + exam
  - Cost: $300-500
  - Benefits: Premium pricing, insurance discounts, credibility
- [ ] **TCIA Accreditation** (Company-level)
  - Cost: $500-2,000
  - Benefits: Marketing advantage, better insurance rates

#### Additional Protections
- [ ] **Umbrella Policy** ($1-5M additional coverage)
  - Cost: $200-600/year per million
- [ ] **Surety Bond** (for commercial clients)
  - Cost: 1-3% of bond amount annually
