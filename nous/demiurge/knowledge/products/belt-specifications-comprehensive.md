# Ardent Belt Product Specifications
*Extracted from product development sessions*

## Belt Dimensions

### Width & Thickness
- **Width**: 1.5" (38mm)
- **Thickness**: 11-13oz Hermann Oak harness leather (~5mm)
- **Construction**: Single layer, no glue layer
- **Waist Sizes Available**: 30, 32, 34, 36, 38

### Length Specifications
- **Turn-back**: 2.5-3" for buckle fold
- **Sizing Holes**: 5 holes at 1" spacing (standard belt sizing)
- **Tip**: English point cut with C.S. Osborne 1.5" cutter

## Leather Specifications

### Primary Leather
- **Source**: Hermann Oak Old World Harness
- **Weight**: 11-13oz 
- **Temper**: Harness temper (tallow-stuffed)
- **Tanning**: Vegetable tanned with oak bark
- **Color**: Natural russet (will darken with age/use)
- **Supplier**: Springfield Leather (140-year tannery)
- **Characteristics**: 
  - Tallow-stuffed during tanning (no additional conditioning needed)
  - Single-layer construction for maximum durability
  - Will develop natural patina over time

### Keeper Leather
- **Source**: Wickett & Craig harness leather  
- **Weight**: 4-6oz
- **Construction**: Floating keeper sewn between buckle attachments
- **Dimensions**: 1/2" - 3/4" width × 4.75-5" length
- **Overlap**: 1/2" when formed into loop
- **Edge Treatment**: Full edge finishing (sand, burnish, wax)

## Thread Specifications

### Thread Details
- **Brand**: Fil au Chinois Lin Câblé 
- **Material**: 100% long-staple French linen
- **Size**: 332 (thickest size, ~0.9mm diameter)
- **Color**: Sans (natural/ecru)
- **Source**: French mill established 1898
- **Characteristics**:
  - Hand-twisted, pre-waxed
  - Traditional saddlery choice
  - Will age naturally with leather
  - Bold visible stitch appropriate for heavy leather

## Hardware Specifications

### Buckle (MAJOR UPDATE)
- **Original**: Buckle Guy solid brass heel bar (#10174) - $4.34 each
- **UPDATED TO**: Abbey England Flat Whole Harness Buckle (B075ALX)
- **Source**: Abbey England, Walsall UK (1832 foundry)
- **Material**: Sand-cast solid brass 
- **Style**: Heavy harness buckle with centre bar
- **Size**: 1.5" / 38mm internal strap width
- **Heritage**: Royal Warrant since 1999
- **Cost**: £11.70 each (~$18 landed)

### Attachment Method (MAJOR CHANGE)
- **Original Plan**: Chicago screws (removable hardware)
- **UPDATED TO**: Stitched attachment (permanent, traditional)
- **Rationale**: Heirloom approach - buckle and belt age together
- **Process**: Buckle attached via saddle-stitched loop around centre bar

### Future Product Line
- **Dress Belt Concept**: Thinner leather with Chicago screws for buckle versatility
- **Current Belt**: Working/sophisticated styling (think Red Wing Iron Rangers)

## Construction Details

### Stitching Method
- **Type**: Hand saddle stitch (no machine stitching)
- **Thread**: 332 Lin Câblé natural
- **Needles**: John James harness needles, size 002
- **Pricking Irons**: KS Blade 3.85mm spacing (10-tooth, 2-tooth)
- **Pattern**: Bold, visible stitches - no groove cut
- **Finishing**: Flattened with Vergez Blanchard saddler hammer

### Buckle Fold Construction  
- **Skiving**: Flesh side tapered to 67% reduction (~4oz at fold)
- **Stitch Distance**: 3-4mm from edge
- **Positioning**: Behind buckle, clear of bend zone
- **Oblong Slot**: 1" C.S. Osborne punch for buckle prong

### Edge Finishing
- **Process**: Hot edge crease → sand → burnish → wax
- **Creasing**: Made on Jupiter M3000 electric creaser with FN2/FN3 tips
- **Sanding**: 220 → 400 → 600 grit progression
- **Edge Treatment**: Gum tragacanth → burnish → Ardent wax
- **Style**: Squared edge (no beveling), traditional harness finish

### Belt Holes
- **Punching**: 4mm round holes (Owden Professional 13-piece set)
- **Spacing**: 1" centers using Belt Ninja template
- **Finishing**: Rod-burnished holes for smooth edges
- **Count**: 5 holes including center size

### Date Stamping
- **Location**: Inside by keeper (discreet marking)
- **Format**: YYYYMMDD (e.g., 20260130)
- **Method**: Brass number stamps on warm leather
- **Purpose**: Quality control and maker's mark

## Finishing Specifications

### Ardent Wax Formula
- **Beeswax**: 1 part (20g test batch)
- **Neatsfoot Oil**: 2 parts (40g test batch) - pure, not compound
- **Lanolin**: 0.5-1 part (10-20g test batch)
- **Application**: Light coat, worked in by hand
- **Purpose**: Protection, slight darkening enhances natural character

### Quality Control
- **Edge Consistency**: Uniform hot crease line
- **Stitch Quality**: Even tension, proper lock-stitching
- **Buckle Fit**: Snug prong clearance, proper fold
- **Overall**: Each belt inspected before shipping

## Philosophy Alignment

### Design Principles
- **"The hand remembers what the mind aims to forget"**
- **Heritage over modern** when it improves durability
- **Minimal finishing** beyond functional requirements
- **Process as proof** of quality, not marketing claims
- **Heirloom quality** through attention and materials, not just claims

- **Abbey England**: 1832 Walsall foundry, Royal Warrant

- **Provenance matters**: Know who makes what you use
