# Ardent Belt - Quick Reference Sheet

## Current Specifications (Final)

### Materials
- **Leather**: Hermann Oak 11-13oz harness (russet)
- **Thread**: Fil au Chinois Lin Câblé 332 Sans  
- **Buckle**: Abbey England Flat Whole Harness (B075ALX)
- **Keeper**: Wickett & Craig harness leather
- **Price**: $150 shipped

### Dimensions
- **Width**: 1.5" 
- **Sizes**: 30, 32, 34, 36, 38
- **Construction**: Single layer, stitched attachment

### Key Tools Required
- KS Blade 3.85mm pricking irons
- John James 002 harness needles
- Made on Jupiter M3000 edge creaser
- C.S. Osborne 1" oblong punch
- Belt Ninja layout template

### Process Summary
1. Cut blank with English point
2. Mark with Belt Ninja template  
3. Skive buckle fold to 67%
4. Hot crease edges (FN2/FN3 tips)
5. Punch holes (4mm) and oblong slot
6. Saddle stitch buckle and keeper
7. Sand/burnish/wax edges
8. Date stamp inside (YYYYMMDD)

- **Thread**: Rocky Mountain Leather Supply
