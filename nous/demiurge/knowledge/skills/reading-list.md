## ESSENTIAL BOOKS (Canonical Texts)

### Al Stohlman - The Foundation
**Priority Order for Study:**

1. **Leathercraft Tools** (1st read) - Complete guide to tool selection, use, and care. Essential before touching any leather.
2. **The Art of Hand Sewing Leather** (1st read) - Indispensable for anyone making gear. Foundation of construction.
3. **The Art of Making Leather Cases** - Fundamental patterns and techniques.
4. **How to Carve Leather** - Carving and tooling fundamentals.
5. **The Stohlman Encyclopedia of Saddle Making** (3 volumes) - Advanced reference work.
6. **The Art of Embossing Leather** - Decorative techniques.

### Tandy Publications - Historical Foundation
- **Basic Leathercraft** (1961) - Available via Internet Archive
- **Adventure in Leather from the Beginning of Time** (1961) - Comic book format history
- **Lacing & Stitching For Leathercraft Book** - Essential techniques
- **Craftool Tech Tips** - Tool usage guides

### Modern Masters
- **Nigel Armitage: "Leathercraft: Traditional Handcrafted Leatherwork Skills and Projects"** - European hand-stitching methods
- **Peter Main: "Stohlman Personal Patterns Portfolio"** (2 volumes) - Compiled original Al & Ann Stohlman patterns

## STRUCTURED LEARNING PATH

### BEGINNER (Months 1-6)
**Foundation Reading:**
1. Al Stohlman: "Leathercraft Tools"
2. Al Stohlman: "The Art of Hand Sewing Leather" 
3. Tandy: "Basic Leathercraft"

**Key Skills to Master:**
- Tool selection and sharpening
- Basic cuts and beveling
- Hand stitching fundamentals
- Simple projects (belts, basic cases)

3. Nigel Armitage: "Leathercraft: Traditional Handcrafted..."

## RESEARCH METHODOLOGY

**Quality Filters:**
- Peer recognition (awards, industry respect)
- Educational impact (structured teaching)
- Technical accuracy (sourced information)
- Historical significance (canonical works)
- Active practice (current masters)

**Avoid:**
- Purely commercial content without educational value
- Unsourced technique claims
- Entertainment-focused channels without serious instruction
- Outdated information not marked as historical reference
