# Thread Options and Specifications

## Primary Thread Choice

### Fil au Chinois Lin Câblé (French Linen)
- **Material:** 100% long-staple flax linen
- **Heritage:** French mill since 1898 (125+ years)
- **Construction:** Hand-twisted by master thread makers
- **Preparation:** Pre-waxed for traditional use
- **Quality:** "First choice of leather craftspeople worldwide for 100+ years"

## Sizing System
**Note:** For Lin Câblé, LOWER numbers = THICKER thread
- 332 (thickest)
- 432 
- 532 
- 632 
- 832 (thinnest)

## Specific Applications

### Belt Construction
- **Size:** 332 Sans (natural/ecru color)
- **Diameter:** ~0.9mm 
- **Appearance:** Bold, visible stitch
- **Compatibility:** Matches 3.85mm KS Blade pricking irons
- **Philosophy:** Classic saddlery look for thick Hermann Oak leather
- **Cost:** ~$25 per spool

### Wallet/Light Goods
- **Size:** 532 Sans
- **Use:** Thinner leather goods
- **Kept as backup option**

## Color Choice: Sans (Natural/Ecru)
- **Traditional:** Saddlery standard
- **Aging:** Darkens naturally with use
- **Philosophy:** "Let the hide speak" - natural materials aging together
- **Alternative colors considered but rejected:** Brown (disappears), Black (too modern)
