## Quality Standards

### Selection Criteria
- Heritage tannery with traditional methods preferred
- Vegetable-tanned over chrome-tanned
- Tallow-stuffed for durability (no synthetic oils)
- Single-layer construction (no glue layers)
- Full-grain, not corrected grain
