## Hardware Philosophy & Selection Criteria

### Quality Standards
- **Material:** Solid brass only (no plated metals)
- **Construction:** Cast, not stamped
- **Provenance:** Know the foundry/manufacturer
- **Heritage:** Traditional methods over modern efficiency
- **Alignment:** Suppliers who understand craft quality

### Philosophy Statement
"Hardware isn't decoration - it's functional architecture. The buckle will outlast the leather if chosen correctly. Solid brass means solid brass. No compromise on material integrity."
