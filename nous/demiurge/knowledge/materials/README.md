## Overview
This directory contains comprehensive materials research and specifications for Ardent Leatherworks, extracted from extensive design sessions and supplier research conducted throughout the brand development process.

## Core Philosophy
"Heritage practices over modern convenience when they produce better durability. Every material choice reflects the principle that attention is a moral act - extended not just to the making, but to the sourcing of what will be made."

## Material Specifications Summary

### Primary Materials (per belt)
- **Hermann Oak leather:** 11-13oz russet harness ($11.29)
- **Abbey England buckle:** Flat Whole Harness 1.5" ($18.00)
- **Fil au Chinois thread:** 332 Sans linen ($0.50)
- **Wickett & Craig keeper:** Harness leather ($1.00)
- **Total materials cost:** $31.79

### Key Suppliers
1. **Hermann Oak Leather Co.** (140+ year tannery)
2. **Abbey England** (1832 foundry, Royal Warrant)  
3. **Fil au Chinois** (French mill since 1898)
4. **Springfield Leather** (Hermann Oak distributor)
5. **Rocky Mountain Leather** (thread supplier)

### Philosophy Integration
Every material choice tested against:
- Heritage precedent and traditional methods
- Quality ceiling in each category
- Natural materials over synthetic
- Honest construction principles  
- Supplier values alignment

## Quality Standards

### Non-Negotiables
- Single-layer construction (no glue bonding)
- Solid brass hardware (no plating)
- Natural fiber thread (linen preferred)
- Traditional tanning methods
- Heritage supplier relationships

### Cost Transparency
Material costs represent ~25% of retail price, with 75% reflecting labor and craftsmanship value. This ratio maintained through honest pricing rather than material markup.
