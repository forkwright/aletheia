## 5. TECHNIQUE-SPECIFIC TOOL REQUIREMENTS

### Saddle Stitching Requirements
- **Fil au Chinois Lin Câblé thread** (sizes 332, 432) - specifically NOT Ritza polyester
- **Proper awl and needles** for hand stitching
- **NO machine stitching** - hand saddle-stitch only for quality

### Edge Finishing
- **Barry King edgers** for consistent edge finishing
- **Burnishing tools** for leather edges and belt holes
- **Minimal finishing approach** - let the hide speak

### Leather Processing
- **Hermann Oak tallow-stuffed leather** - no synthetic conditioning needed
- **Ardent wax mixture** (beeswax, neatsfoot, lanolin) - custom finish blend
- **Vegetable tanning only** - no chrome tanning

### Hardware Standards
- **Solid brass only** - no plated metals
- **Heel bar buckles without rollers** - traditional design
- **Chicago screws with dome caps** - removable, serviceable hardware

### Dye Work (Limited Batches)
- **Natural dye recipes documented** in RECIPES.md
- **Mordant chemistry** using iron for color fixing
- **Batch production** rather than made-to-order dyeing
