## ETSY (ðŸŸ¡ INCREASINGLY DIFFICULT)

### Reality
- **Fees:** 6.5% transaction + $0.20 listing + 3-4% payment = ~10% total
- **2025:** Many sellers report worst year yet
- **Problem:** Algorithm now favors app traffic; flooded with Chinese goods reposted from Amazon

### What Works
- Items under $100 with "free shipping" built into price
- Heavily SEO-optimized listings
- Consistent posting schedule, seasonal items

### What Doesn't
- Basic SEO and hoping for the best

## OWN WEBSITE (ðŸŸ¢ BEST LONG-TERM)

### Reality
- **ROI:** $22 return for every $1 spent
- **Conversion:** 14.6% organic vs 1.7% paid
- **Timeline:** 3-6 months minimum for SEO results
- **Investment:** $500-2000 upfront, $200-500/month ongoing

### What Works
- Local SEO: "handmade leather goods [city]"
- Content marketing around craftsmanship/process
- Email list building from day one
- Strong reviews and testimonials
