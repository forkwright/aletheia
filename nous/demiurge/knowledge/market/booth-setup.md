## DISPLAY PRINCIPLES FOR LEATHER GOODS

### Height Variation
- **Eye level:** Featured belt on display stand
- **Mid level:** Valet trays, laid flat with one elevated
- **Table level:** Additional inventory, business cards

### Let Them Touch
- Have a **sample belt** people can pick up and feel
- Quality sells itself when they feel the leather weight
- Don't hover â€” let them explore

### Less is More
- Don't overcrowd the table
- Space between items = perceived value
- **Abundance without clutter**

### Tell the Story
- Small sign: *"Hand saddle-stitched. Hermann Oak leather since 1881. Solid brass hardware."*
- Let the materials speak â€” no need for hard sell

### Lighting (if indoor/evening)
- Battery-powered LED strip lights
- Spotlight on featured piece
- Warm tone, not harsh white
