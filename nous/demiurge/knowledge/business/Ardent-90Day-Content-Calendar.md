# Summary & Implementation

## Total Time Investment
- **Average per week:** 10.2 hours
- **Range:** 8.5-13.5 hours  
- **Within budget:** 8-12 hours (some weeks require flex)

## Platform Distribution
- **Instagram:** 70% (primary platform, daily content)
- **Reddit:** 20% (educational depth, community building)
- **Pinterest:** 10% (visual discovery, lifestyle content)

## Content Type Breakdown
- **Process Reels:** 40% (core brand content)
- **Educational Static:** 25% (technical depth)
- **Story Series:** 20% (behind-scenes, quick moments)
- **Lifestyle/Beauty:** 15% (aspirational content)

## Success Metrics
- **Authentic documentation** over performance
- **Community education** over sales pressure
- **Philosophical resonance** over broad appeal
- **Quality consistency** over quantity

## Implementation Notes
- Document real work, don't create content work
- Let philosophy guide content, not algorithm demands
- Build for those who "get it" — let others leave
- Maintain craft focus — content serves making, not vice versa
