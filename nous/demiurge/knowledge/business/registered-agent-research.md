# Registered Agent Research

*Researched: 2026-01-30*
*Decision: Northwest Registered Agent*

---

## What Cody Chose

**Northwest Registered Agent**
- LLC Formation: $39 + $310 TX state fee = $349
- Registered Agent: First year FREE, then $125/year
- Total Year 1: $349

---

## What's Included with Northwest

**Registered Agent Service:**
- Their address on public record (not home address)
- Same-day document scanning & forwarding
- Service of process handling
- State correspondence forwarding
- Compliance reminders (annual report deadlines)

**Business Identity Bundle (free with RA):**
- Business address
- Domain name
- Basic website
- Business email
- Business phone number
- Northwest pays renewal fees as long as RA service active

---

## Why Northwest Won

1. Privacy by Default — home address stays off public records
2. No upsells or surprise fees
3. 25+ years operating
4. Doesn't sell customer data
5. Includes business identity bundle free
