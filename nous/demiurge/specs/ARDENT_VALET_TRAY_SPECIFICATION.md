## CONSTRUCTION PROCESS

### Phase 1: Preparation (45 minutes)
1. **Cut blank** — 12" × 10" rectangle from Hermann Oak 6-7oz
2. **Mark layout** — Center tray outline (8" × 6") with 3/8" margin
3. **Mark corners** — 1.5" squares at each corner position
4. **Cut corners** — Remove corner squares with L'Indispensable
5. **Test fit** — Fold corners to verify clean 90° angles

### Phase 2: Edge Preparation (30 minutes)
6. **Sand edges** — Progress 220 → 400 → 600 grit
7. **Apply gum tragacanth** — Light coat for burnishing base
8. **Initial burnish** — Smooth with wood burnisher
9. **Mark stitch lines** — KS Blade for consistent spacing

### Phase 3: Stitching (90 minutes)
10. **Punch holes** — All four corner seams using KS Blade 3.85mm
11. **Cut thread** — 4 pieces, each 4× seam length
12. **Thread needles** — John James 002 harness needles
13. **Stitch corners** — Saddle stitch each of 4 corner seams
14. **Lock stitches** — Back-stitch final 2-3 holes per seam
15. **Trim threads** — Flush cut and flatten with mallet

### Phase 4: Finishing (45 minutes)
16. **Final edge work** — Sand any rough spots from stitching
17. **Burnish edges** — Complete edge burnishing process
18. **Apply finish** — Light coat of Ardent wax if desired
19. **Form tray** — Set final shape and check corner integrity
20. **Quality control** — Inspect stitching, edges, dimensions
21. **Date stamp** — YYYYMMDD on bottom exterior
22. **Final inspection** — Full QC checklist

**Total construction time:** 3.5-4 hours

## QUALITY STANDARDS

### Acceptance Criteria

| Attribute | Standard |
|-----------|----------|
| **Stitch consistency** | Even spacing ±0.5mm, no skipped holes |
| **Corner integrity** | Clean 90° angles, no gaps or puckering |
| **Edge finish** | Smooth burnish, no roughness |
| **Leather selection** | Minimal scarring, consistent grain |
| **Overall fit** | Items sit flat, tray maintains form |

### Defect Classification

- **Critical:** Broken stitches, torn corners, major scarring
- **Major:** Uneven stitching, edge roughness, minor tears
- **Minor:** Small scars, slight color variation, minor edge imperfections

**QC standard:** Zero critical defects, <2 major defects, <5 minor defects
