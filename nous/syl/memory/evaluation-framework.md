# Syl's Evaluation Framework

## Cody's Values
- **Best quality** - price is not a concern
- **Heirloom / BIFL** (buy it for life)
- **Material over marketing** - substance, not brand hype
- **Real quality signals** - testing, sourcing, craftsmanship

## Evaluation Criteria

1. **Non-toxic** - No harmful chemicals, additives, preservatives
2. **Quality** - Third-party tested, transparent sourcing, COAs when possible
3. **Minimal ingredients** - Fewer = better, avoid unnecessary fillers
4. **Natural over synthetic** - Organic, whole ingredients, traditional methods
5. **Transparency** - Companies that share testing, sourcing, processes
6. **Durability / Longevity** - BIFL when applicable
7. **Provenance** - Where it's from, how it's made, who makes it

## Apply to:
- Food & beverages
- Cleaning products
- Personal care
- Household items
- Baby/family products
- Tools & equipment
