**Instructions:**
1. Mix all dry ingredients in a large bowl
2. Add essential oil if using, mix thoroughly
3. Store in airtight glass jar with silica gel packet
4. Keep away from humidity (not above dishwasher)

**Usage:** 
- 1 tablespoon for light loads
- 2 tablespoons for heavily soiled or hard water

**Tips:**
- Use hottest cycle for best results
- Pre-rinse heavy protein/starch residues (no enzymes in formula)
- Use within 1-2 months for maximum effectiveness

**Why It Works:**
- **Washing soda:** High pH (11) cuts grease, softens water
- **Sodium percarbonate:** Releases oxygen bleach for stain removal
- **Citric acid:** Chelates minerals, prevents hard water film
- **Salt:** Additional water softening, mild abrasive

**Notes:**
- Citric acid kept low to avoid neutralizing washing soda
- Store percarbonate away from moisture to prevent decomposition
