# Sudan Genocide — Source Index

## Status
- Active genocide in Darfur. RSF (Rapid Support Forces) conducting ethnic purges.
- World's largest displacement crisis: 12M+ displaced
- Genocide Watch issued alert. US Holocaust Memorial Museum warned.
- Near-zero Western media coverage.

## Key Events
- **El Fasher fell to RSF**: Became a "crime scene" per UN. Mass graves, secret detention centers.
  - Source: Sudan Tribune investigation, UN News 2026-01
- **RSF drone attack Feb 7, 2026**: 24 displaced civilians killed fleeing fighting in Rahad, central Sudan
  - Source: Guardian 2026-02-07, Al Jazeera 2026-02-07
- **ICC prosecutor to Security Council**: Atrocities "repeated town by town"
  - Crimes: rape, arbitrary detention, executions, mass graves — often filmed and celebrated by perpetrators
  - Source: UN News 2026-01

## RSF Atrocities (Documented)
- Genocide, mass killings, ethnically targeted violence against non-Arab communities
- Sexual assault, forced displacement, systematic looting
- Secret detention centers: murder, rape, torture, starvation, financial extortion
- Perpetrators filming and uploading atrocities as content
- Source: Sudan Tribune, HRW World Report 2026, IGAD condemnation

## War Context
- Civil war since April 2023: SAF vs RSF
- RSF descends from Janjaweed militias that killed hundreds of thousands in 2000s
- Kordofan Campaign intensified since Oct 2025 after El Fasher fell
- Both sides attacking humanitarian workers and blocking aid

## International Response
- IGAD condemned RSF crimes for first time (2 weeks ago)
- ICC investigating
- Practical intervention: none
- US withdrawn from engagement (USAID gutted, UN defunded)
