# Website and Review Intelligence Gathering
Systematically research a company by fetching official pages and aggregating third-party reviews and discussions.

## When to Use
When you need to comprehensively understand a company's offerings, policies, and reputation through both official sources and independent user feedback. Useful for due diligence, comparison research, or building detailed company profiles.

## Steps
1. Enable web_fetch tool
2. Fetch the company's homepage to identify main offerings
3. Fetch key company pages (About, FAQ, product categories, policies)
4. Enable web_search tool
5. Search for independent reviews and discussions on relevant platforms
6. Search for specific product/service reviews and complaints
7. Fetch detailed review articles from trusted sources
8. Fetch community discussions (Reddit, forums) for unfiltered user perspectives

## Tools Used
- web_fetch: to retrieve complete content from official company pages and detailed review articles
- web_search: to locate independent reviews, discussions, and third-party coverage of the company and specific products
