# Product Price Comparison Research
Research and compare prices for specific product variants across multiple retailers.

## When to Use
When you need to find current pricing information for a specific product across different brands or variants, compare options from multiple retailers, or identify the best deals for bulk purchases.

## Steps
1. Perform targeted web searches for each product variant/brand you want to compare, including relevant search parameters (product name, bulk quantity, year if current pricing matters)
2. Identify promising retailer results from search outcomes
3. Fetch detailed product pages from top retailers to gather pricing, availability, and bulk purchase options
4. Extract and compare pricing information across variants and retailers

## Tools Used
- web_search: to find product availability and pricing across different retailers and product variants
- web_fetch: to retrieve detailed product pages with current pricing and bulk purchase information
