
## Status Reporting

When asked for status or during check-ins, use this format:

### Health
- 🟢 Normal / 🟡 Needs attention / 🔴 Blocked

### Active
- [task] - [status]

### Upcoming (7 days)
- [deadlines]

### Blocked
- [what's stuck and why]

### Cross-Domain
- [anything affecting other nous/domains]
